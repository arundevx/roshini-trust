Our Story

Connect with Love

8+

Years of Service

A pillar of support in In all over tamilnadu

At Roshini Charitable Trust, our foundation is built on the simple yet powerful philosophy: "Connect with Love." For over 8 years, we have been a pillar of support In all over tamilnadu, serving those forgotten by society.

Our mission spans across providing meals to the homeless, securing the education of children, and restoring nature through our green initiatives. We act as a vital bridge between compassionate donors and the most vulnerable lives, ensuring that every act of kindness creates a lasting, visible impact.

Our Initiatives

Women Empowerment Programs

Women empowerment programs are organized initiatives designed to improve the social, economic, and political status of women. They typically offer education, vocational training, micro-loans, and legal support to promote self-reliance, gender equality, and leadership opportunities. A variety of highly actionable and localized empowerment programs, entrepreneurship initiatives, and safety frameworks are available.

Rural Upliftment

It focuses on the upliftment of rural women and children through health, education, livelihood support, and environmental initiatives in the all over tamilnadu.

Livelihood & Skill Training

The NGO runs vocational training programs—such as skill-building in beauty care and tailoring—to promote economic sustainability for rural women. Vocational Training on Beauty Care for Women.

Our Journey

A life-changing movement

"Our story began 8 years ago as a small act of kindness in all over tamil nadu. What started as a simple mission to feed a few hungry souls has now evolved into a life-changing movement."

The Beginning

Launched with a primary focus on roadside food distribution in all over tamil nadu, starting with a handful of dedicated members.

Growing Impact

Our network grew to hundreds of volunteers in all over tamil nadu, spreading compassion and practical help.

Expanding Horizons

Proudly supported the education of 130+ children, planted hundreds of trees, and adopted 80+ elderly people in need.

Today

We stand as a 24/7 support system, managing food rescue missions, animal welfare, and emergency blood coordination.
