Volunteer With Us

Join the Mission of
Love & Impact

At Roshini Charitable Trust, we believe that while one person can make a difference, a team can create a revolution. Your skills, time, and passion are the greatest gifts you can give to the community. Whether you are a student, a working professional, or a retiree, there is a place for you in our family.

Explore Our Volunteer Roles

Choose the one that best fits your talent

1. Donor Relationship Manager

The Role: Act as the bridge between our supporters and the trust.

Responsibilities: Engaging with donors, providing project updates, expressing gratitude, and ensuring transparency in how their contributions are used.

Best for: People with excellent communication and people skills.

2. Mobile Media Crew (Content Creators)

The Role: The digital eyes and ears of the trust.

Responsibilities: Capturing high-quality photos and videos of our field activities using your smartphone. Editing quick reels and stories (InShot/VN) to showcase our impact on social media.

Best for: Aspiring photographers, videographers, and social media enthusiasts.

3. Field Crew (Ground Force)

The Role: The hands-on team that makes things happen.

Responsibilities: Direct involvement in food distribution drives, sapling plantation, animal feeding, and organizing local community events.

Best for: Those who love physical activity and direct interaction with the community.

4. Emergency Response Team

The Role: Our rapid action force.

Responsibilities: Coordinating emergency blood donation requests and managing food rescue calls from wedding halls or events.

Best for: Individuals who are quick to respond and good at coordination.

Why Volunteer with Roshini?
